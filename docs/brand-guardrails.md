# secunit, llc brand guardrails

## Core guardrails

- Keep the primary wordmark as `secunit, llc` in all lowercase.
- Default to dark-mode-first palettes; optimize contrast for dark surfaces first.
- Build each concept around a single primary hue (blue, bright green, or burnt orange).
- Favor clean geometric forms and avoid decorative complexity.
- Keep logo treatment flat; no heavy gradients, 3D effects, or glows in base marks.
- Preserve generous spacing and avoid cramped lockups.
- Ensure body and UI text meet WCAG AA contrast targets.
- Prefer restrained trust cues over flashy visuals.
- Maintain consistent spacing, type rhythm, and interaction patterns across pages.
- Keep tone direct and plain: security-forward without jargon or hype.

## Scoring rubric (1-5)

| Criterion | 1 | 3 | 5 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Dark-mode coherence | Inconsistent on dark | Works in dark mode | Dark-first and polished |
| Security/trust signal | Generic or unclear | Neutral | Strongly credible and calm |
| Minimal timelessness | Trend-heavy | Balanced | Clean and durable |
| Concept clarity | Mixed style cues | Mostly coherent | Clear single direction |
| Brand alignment | Off voice/shape | Mostly aligned | Fully aligned to secunit, llc |

## Copy voice

Use clear, direct language that explains privacy controls and verification steps in plain terms.
